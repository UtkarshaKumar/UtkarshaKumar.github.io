About

I've owned and led product direction on a Fortune 500 enterprise platform — holding business architecture authority, guiding a large BA/QA organisation, and turning around a major production crisis by restructuring delivery from the ground up. I drive outcomes through product influence, not positional authority — aligning leadership, engineering, and peer product teams around the decisions that move the platform. When something is structurally broken, I get called in.

The pattern repeats across six regulated industries: healthcare, insurance, energy, wellness, banking, and financial services. Every engagement involved regulated data at scale, complex customer hierarchies, and high-stakes migration where failure wasn't an option. That's not consulting breadth — it's a specialisation in the hardest class of enterprise product problems.

On AI: I don't just ship AI features. I build the measurement infrastructure that tells you whether they worked. Five production agents with a validated 19% efficiency gain. An LLM eval harness at 75% F1 and 100% deadline accuracy. An Agentic Commerce strategy published before Gartner named the category. Builder first, strategist second.

Recognition: Top performer at Deloitte (95th percentile since 2018). Business Maven Award. Presented at Deloitte's Global Commerce Summit 2025 and published on KX. 10+ Applause Awards.
