The B2B Competitive Intelligence Agent That Runs on Your Machine

Originally published on Notion ↗

The Problem Nobody Talks About

Every B2B procurement team and product manager we know shares the same frustration: they know their competitors are pricing differently, but they have no systematic way to track it.

The options available today are blunt instruments:

  • Hire someone to manually check supplier sites and update a spreadsheet
  • Buy an expensive SaaS platform that gives you dashboards for industries and geographies it was pre-built for
  • Build a custom scraper that breaks the moment a site changes its HTML

None of these scale. None of them handle the messy reality of B2B supply chains, where the same product can appear under ten different names across eight platforms, and "price" means something different on Alibaba vs. IndiaMart vs. a direct supplier portal.

This is where Universal Market Intelligence(UMI) like solution can come to solve this differently

What Is UMI?

UMI (Universal Market Intelligence) is a local-first, AI-powered competitive intelligence agent built for B2B product managers and procurement teams.

It runs entirely on your machine—no cloud subscription, no data leaving your network. Download it, run one script, and within minutes you'll have a full competitive intelligence command center in your browser.

The core promise is simple: tell UMI what you sell and who you are, and it figures out the rest.

How It Works: Intelligence That Starts at Onboarding

Most competitive intelligence tools assume you already know your competitors. UMI flips this.

On first launch, UMI asks four questions:

  • What is your company name?
  • What industry do you operate in?
  • What does your business do?
  • What product categories do you sell?

That's it. From this profile, UMI's AI identifies which platforms and marketplaces are relevant to your business—Amazon, Alibaba, IndiaMart, Flipkart, or niche industry directories—dynamically, based on your sector.

Not a hardcoded list. Not a dropdown. The AI figures out where your competitors actually operate.

This platform discovery is persistent. Every subsequent action—price refreshes, competitor searches, AI chat—draws on this profile. The system understands your business and applies that context automatically.
